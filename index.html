This site, the creation of a single human being, is many things. It’s a portfolio of my travels, my writing and my creative work. What I want to offer here the pursuit of truth, creative expression, experience and insight in a way that is not compromised, as much as possible when visiting foreign countries...

All the content here is created, designed and arranged by me and nothing here is designed to gain profits. I feel compelled to speak out as well as to share some wisdom gained through my travel experiences, something that most websites are in conflict with due to the goal of a tailored presentation of something to gain views or income. In this way this is a unique resource on the internet...

The main point here is to give my voice a platform and provide a virtual artist’s studio of sorts. The main written feature here is the tale of my Indian adventures “Beans’ Bodh Gaya and Beyond.” I also hope to showcase other posts, written articles, photography, video links, drawings, digital illustrations, crude animations and audio and music that I have put together. Storage of all this on this site is proving difficult. Bear with me as I figure this out...

The name Mixter E was a nickname I earned through my musical efforts with field recordings, a drum kit, a Roland SP 808 groove sampler, and other musicians. Mixter E also refers to my first name and to a constant exploration of the mysteries of the self and life...
