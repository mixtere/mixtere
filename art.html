Art

Artist: Ian McErlane

Clicking on an image will open it in a new tab. Included here is everthing from basic sketches to full vector illustrations. Showcased here is more than a decade of work. Some of the work was created for animation and interactive games. I succeeded in a few crude animations but the complexity of game design escaped me. Some of these images are one-off's, others are part of a project series. I am at a point where I no longer have a working tablet or a desire to trace over drawings in a program. Therefore there are experiments here that are scanned sketches that were then converted into vector art through software. Recurring themes are dogs, social commentary, relationships and gender roles, potty humor, the grip of technology, and just raw creative stuff.
